Book Mate

Branding / UX / UI

Brief

This is an app for people who want to understand a book in depth or from various angles. A lot of background information is needed to properly understand a book. People voluntarily upload information found on the Internet to solve each other's curiosity.

Objective

To enhance a reading experience by providing a book related resources such as Podcasts, Youtube, and visual/auditory materials.
