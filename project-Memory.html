Memory

Branding / UX / UI

Brief

This is a website for people who work for terminally ill patients. After the death of the patient, non-family members, such as hospice staff and volunteers, do not have a proper opportunity to say goodbye to the patients they cared for. They need to resolve grief over the patient's death for their mental health and sustainability of work. The website is a place where people express their condolences on the patient’s death.

Objective

To express and share users emotions, thoughts, and stories related to the patients. Expected users are mainly Health care provider staffs, Hospice staffs, and volunteers.
